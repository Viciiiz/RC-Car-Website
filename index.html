Super Speed, Super Torque.

Introducing RC Version 1.0

With its 2.3-inch wheels, this first model has a great Speed and large Torque driven by its 8V motor. Once you get your hands on the remote control, you will feel the smoothness of the car despite its heavy weight.

Quick Story

This RC car was an afternoon project that came to my mind when I was watching a video about NASA's Perseverance rover. I began to draw the rover and thought about making one until I realized I didn't have enough motors at hand. So, I thought, why not make a RC car that would have more or less the same functionalities as the rover and push it to its limits? And so, the RC Version 1.0 was born...
